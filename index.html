About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in the CSE department at Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Yusu Wang. My research interests are graph representation learning and topological data analysis. Previously, I obtained my master at Stony Brook University, where Prof. Joseph Mitchell and Prof. Jie Gao introduced me to computational geometry and differntial privacy.
