About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the CSE department at the University of California, San Diego, advised by Prof. Yusu Wang. My research interests are graph representation learning and topological data analysis. Previously, I obtained my master's degree in Applied Mathematics at Stony Brook University, where Prof. Joseph Mitchell and Prof. Jie Gao introduced me to computational geometry and differential privacy.
